About Us

HeartField is a nonprofit dedicated to environmental sustainability and community through nature-based initiatives. Focused on conservation, reforestation, and sustainable farming, it aims to protect the planet for future generations. The name reflects the connection between compassion (Heart) and the natural world (Field)
